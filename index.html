OSIN is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting transparency and accountability in politics through the use of open source data and analysis.
We believe that open access to data and the ability to analyze it are key to a healthy and democratic society.
That's why we use technology and techniques to collect, process, and analyze political data from a variety of sources.
Whether it's tracking voting patterns, monitoring campaign spending, or analyzing public opinion, we aim to provide insights that are accessible, actionable, and impactful.
Our work is guided by a commitment to impartiality, transparency, and open collaboration. We work with experts in the fields of data science, politics, and journalism to produce high-quality analysis that is free from bias and accessible to all
Our research is published under open licenses, so that others can build upon our work and advance the field.
If you're interested in politics, data, or the role of technology in society, we invite you to get involved. Join our community of volunteers, activists, and supporters to help us drive change and make a positive impact. Together, we can build a more informed and engaged citizenry, and a better future for all.
